## Solution

**Feature**: Highlighted accounts tab.

**User Stories**:

### User Story 01
**Story**:
As an Instagram user, I want to browse highlighted influencer accounts to follow ones that match my interests.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
1. Given that I am logged in as a user, when I click on the highlights tab, then I see a list of recommended accounts based on my follow history.
2. Given that I am not logged in as a user, when I click on the highlights tab, then I see a list of top accounts from different categories.

___
### User Story 02
As an Instagram user, I want to filter what types of accounts are shown in the highlights so I only see ones relevant to my interests.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
1. Given that I am not logged in as a user, when I click the filter option in the highlighted accounts tab, then I see a list of filtration options that I can choose.
2. Given that I am logged in as a user, when I filter the options in the highlighted accounts tab, then my choices are saved to my account to apply to future browsing automatically.
___
### User Story 03
As an art lover, I want to see the top or recommended artist Instagram accounts to follow the ones whose art I like.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
1. Given that I follow many artist accounts, when I open the highlighted accounts tab, then I see recommendations for top and upcoming artists that I have not yet followed.
2. Given that I follow many artist accounts, when I open the highlighted accounts tab, then I see the top recommended accounts for artists that match the type of artists I follow (painter, comic, musician, etc).
___
### User Story 04
As an Instagram business/influencer account user, I want to see a list of requirements of how to get highlighted so I can get my account showcased.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
1. "Get Highlighted" option is clicked/selected.
2. Business account status is verified.
3. The type of business/influencer is selected.
4. Checklist of highlight criteria to fulfil is displayed.
5. Currently fulfilled criteria are checkmarked.
___
### User Story 05
As an Instagram business account user, I want to edit and control what gets shown in my highlight so I can optimize my highlights views conversion rate to follower.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
1. User is logged in to a business Instagram account.
2. "Modify Highlight" option in business user account settings is clicked.
3. User selects which of their posts may be randomly displayed in their highlight.
4. User saves selection to apply it.
